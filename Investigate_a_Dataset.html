Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

in order to complete the project i will be using 10,000 movies from TMDb .

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including revenue, and user ratings. it consists of 21 columns and about 10867 Rows (one for each movie):

The 21 columns are:

  • ID
  • IMDb_ID
  • Popularity
  • Budget
  • Revenue
  • Original_Title
  • Cast
  • Homepage
  • Director
  • Tagline
  • Keywords
  • Overview
  • Runtime
  • Genres
  • Production_Companies
  • Release_Date
  • Vote_Count
  • Vote_Average
  • Release_Year
  • Budget_Adj
  • Revenue_Adj
### Questions for Analysis
  • 1- What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?
  • 2- What are the top 10 movies of all times?
  • 3- What are the top 10 directors of all times according to their successful movies?
  • 4- What kind of movies are most likely to succeed?
  • 5- How budget is correlated to revenue?

Data Cleaning¶

In this part, we try to solve the following problems.

Problems with data¶

after we have examined a sample of the data we can notice that:

  • There are some outliers that must be dropped off
  • There are some missing values that must be dropped off
  • The currency for the revenue is not specified but we can solve this by assuming it's in dollars since this is more reasonable
  • The "release date" column has a string-type data which must be converted to a datetime type
  • The "budget" and the "revenue" column has a float-type data which must be converted to a int-type
  • There is one duplicated data which must be dropped off
  • The row can be specified by either "id" or "imdb_id", so we can get rid of one of them

    Conclusions¶

    Summary¶

    we can summarize our findings in the folowing points:

    • movies having high budget are more likely to earn high revenue
    • Not all movies that earn high revenue are highly rated
    • The most genres of movies to be successful are: Action, Drama, Comedy, and thriller
    • Some of the best movies are low budget movies, and some of them are old movies from the twentieth century

    Limitations¶

    There are some few limitations in the data that may make affect the accuracy of some results and predictions:

    • The null data that was dropped off could have some effect on the results
    • The changing of the budget and revenue range over time (outliers) may have caused some biased conclusions
